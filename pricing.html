Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common pricing questions

What's included in the one-time payment?
The one-time payment includes complete setup, training, integration, and the support period specified in your plan. There are no recurring fees unless you choose our optional monthly maintenance plan.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. We'll apply your previous payment toward the new plan, and you'll only pay the difference. Upgrades are typically completed within 24 hours.
Do you offer custom solutions?
Absolutely! If your needs aren't covered by our standard plans, contact us for a custom quote. We'll work with you to create a tailored solution that fits your specific requirements.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your AI agent within the first week, we'll refund your payment in full.
