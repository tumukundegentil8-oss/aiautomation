Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up an AI agent?
Most AI agents are set up within 2-5 business days, depending on the complexity of your requirements and the package you choose.
Can the AI agent integrate with my existing systems?
Yes, our AI agents can integrate with popular CRM systems, calendar applications, and other business tools. We'll discuss your specific needs during the consultation.
What kind of training is required for my team?
We provide comprehensive training for your team to manage and update the AI agent. Most clients find the interface intuitive and require minimal training.
Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance?
Yes, all our packages include a support period. We also offer monthly maintenance plans for ongoing updates and optimization.
What happens if the AI doesn't understand a customer question?
Our AI agents are designed to gracefully handle unfamiliar questions by either asking for clarification or escalating to a human agent, ensuring no customer query goes unanswered.
