Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our AI agents

How long does it take to set up my AI agent?
Most AI agents are set up within 2-5 business days. Basic agents typically take 2 days, while more complex Business Assistant setups may take up to 5 days. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during your onboarding call.
What information do you need to set up my AI agent?
We need your business information, common customer questions, service/product details, calendar integration preferences (if applicable), and any specific workflows you want to automate. We'll guide you through providing all necessary information during the setup process.
How do I train my AI agent on new questions?
You can train your AI agent through our user-friendly dashboard. Simply go to the Training section, add new questions and answers, and the AI will learn immediately. We also offer training sessions to show you how to optimize your agent's responses.
Can I customize the AI agent's personality and tone?
Yes! You can customize your AI agent's tone, personality, and response style through our dashboard. Choose from predefined personalities or create a custom tone that matches your brand voice perfectly.
What happens if the AI doesn't understand a customer question?
When the AI encounters an unfamiliar question, it will either ask for clarification or escalate to a human agent (if configured). All unanswered questions are logged in your dashboard for review and training, helping your AI agent continuously improve.
My AI agent is giving incorrect responses. What should I do?
If your AI agent gives incorrect responses, you can correct it directly in the dashboard. The system will learn from corrections. For persistent issues, contact our support team, and we'll help optimize your agent's training data.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for larger projects. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your AI agent within the first week, we'll provide a full refund. After 7 days, refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.
